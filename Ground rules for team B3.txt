Ground RulesTeam B3A. Rules for developing a great team environment * Strict observance of the Criminal Code of Canada.* Strict observance of University of Saskatchewan Code of Conduct for Non-academic Matters. * Be respectful to each other and make a comfortable team environment. * Zero tolerance towards intolerance. * Never being judgemental and avoid using any abusive luanuage. B. Rules for improving team work efficiency* Be on time and inform the teammates at least 6 hours ahead of the scheduled time if unable to attend the meeting. * Be fully prepared for each meeting. * Be responsible for one’ own part of work.* Remember to make, allow and encourage contribution to each group meeting.* Advocate using democratic strategies on conflict resolution.
